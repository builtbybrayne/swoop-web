The W Trek in Patagonia.

Five days, four nights, roughly 80 km on foot through Torres del Paine National Park. The W-shape covers the park's three defining valleys — the French, the British, and the towers themselves. Moderate-to-challenging; fit walkers only.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 Arrive at Paine Grande refuge. Short afternoon walk to Lago Grey viewpoint if weather allows.
  2. Day 2 Up the French Valley to the British viewpoint. Hanging glacier views, granite amphitheatre.
  3. Day 3 Traverse to Los Cuernos. Undulating ridge walk along Lago Nordenskjöld.
  4. Day 4 Dawn ascent to the base of the Torres. The whole-trip-in-one-morning payoff.
  5. Day 5 Out to Hotel las Torres. Transfer to Puerto Natales.
